VOLTRON

giant robot lions make the brain chemicals happy


voltron first premiered back in 2016. i started watching in 2018 just before season 6 released, and the show ended in 2018. it is now 2023 and it still somehow lives rent free in my mind.

if i had known back then that i would spend the next 4-5 years of my life obsessing over a kids tv show about giant space robot cats, i wouldve decided to not watch it. but here we fucking are.

4 teenagers + 1 tired 20 something end up in outer space through the power of a blue lion and a wormhole. they find a castle with 2 random aliens called alteans. shenanigans and an intergalactic war ensue. such a banger fucking premise yet the last two seasons went to complete shit AND ITS STILL ONE OF MY TOP SHOWS OF ALL TIME. HOW? I WILL NEVER KNOW. I OWN THE FIRST 2 SEASONS ON DVD AND I WANNA GET THE REST OF 4-6 CAUSE 7-8 DIDNT GET A DVD RELEASE. I LOVE THE DUMBASS CHARACTERS SO FUCKING MUCH.
